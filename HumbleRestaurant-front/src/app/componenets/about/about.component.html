About MunchCare

"MunchCare is "Healthcare by Exchange for Someone Else's Needs" founded by three friends and graduates of the University of Texas at Austin: Michael Atwood, Gregory Hsiao, and Mebin Mathew. Our website is currently in its testing phase and we are currently in the application process for nonprofit organizational status.





Our Goal

The mission of MunchCare is to help subsidize healthcare costs for those in need. Specifically, we will be funding for patients who have any kind of cancer, heart disease, or genetic disorder. We plan on raising funds for our mission through other businesses in exchange for advertisement.





Additional

In order to attract users to our site and by extension affiliated businesses of MunchCare, users of MunchCare.com will be able to accrue points whenever they purchase and send us receipts from businesses advertised by MunchCare. These points users accrue will be able to be exchanged for items such as TVs, clothes, concert tickets, computers, or other things that MunchCare's user base is most interested in. If you have any additional questions, please email us at MunchCare@MunchCare.com."
